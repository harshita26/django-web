Description

Goa owes its magnetism to its sandy beaches extended along the sun-drenched coastline. The climate is barmy. The perfect blend of sun, sand and sea makes the tourists fell in love with this amazing destination. The warm hospitality of the people and the breathtaking panoramas render a congenial travel experience to the tourists. Without a doubt, Goa is one of the most sought after beach destinations in the world and it is frequented by vacationers from all around the blue planet. When this tour is mixed with the opulence of the commercial capital of India- Mumbai, the delight becomes more profound and the trip experience becomes never to be forgotten. This trip jumbles the tour of Mumbai with Goa and caters a complete beach tour. The combination of these two sublime tourist destinations is something that an avid vacationer can't afford to miss.
